Definition 3.1.1. Polynomial Function.

A polynomial function of is a function of the form
\begin{equation*} f(x) = a_nx^n + a_{n-1}x^{n-1} + \cdots + a_1x + a_0, \end{equation*}
where \(n\) is an integer, \(a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_n\) are numbers and \(a_n \neq 0\text{.}\)
We say \(f\) is a polynomial of degree \(n\) and write \(\deg{f} = n\text{.}\) The numbers \(a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_n\) are called the coefficient of \(1, x, \ldots, x^n\text{,}\) respectively.
