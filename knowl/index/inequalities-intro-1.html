We have focused primarily on solving equations of the form
\begin{equation*} f(x) = 0 \end{equation*}
where \(f\) is a polynomial function in the variable \(x\text{.}\) We now turn to polynomial inequalities which are relations of the form
  • \(f(x) \lt 0\text{,}\)
  • \(f(x) \leq 0\text{,}\)
  • \(f(x) > 0\text{,}\) or
  • \(\displaystyle f(x) \geq 0.\)
