Theorem 5.1.7. Properties of Exponential Functions.

Assume \(a \neq 1\) is a positive integer.
  1. \(\displaystyle \Exp{a}(0) = a^0 = 1.\)
  2. For all real numbers \(x\) and \(x^\prime\text{,}\)
    \begin{equation*} \Exp{a}(x + x^\prime) = a^{x+x^\prime} = a^x a^{x^\prime} = \Exp{a}(x)\Exp{a}(x^\prime). \end{equation*}
  3. For all real numbers \(x\) and \(x^\prime\text{,}\)
    \begin{equation*} \Exp{a}(x - x^\prime) = \Exp{a}^{x-x^\prime} = \frac{a^x}{a^{x^\prime}} = \frac{\Exp{a}(x)}{\Exp{a}(x^\prime)}. \end{equation*}
