Definition 5.3.1. Composition of Functions.

Assume \(f \colon A \to \R\) and \(g \colon B \to \R\) are functions with \(f(A) \subseteq B\text{.}\) The composition of \(g\) with \(f\) is the function \(g \circ f \colon A \to \R\) defined by
\begin{equation*} (g \circ f)(x) = g\left(f\left(x\right)\right). \end{equation*}
