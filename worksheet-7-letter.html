    Worksheet Representation of Lines
    A4US

    Objectives

    • Determine the slope of a line through two points.
    • Represent a line using
      • Slope-Intercept Form,
      • Point-Slope Form, and
      • Standard Form.

    1.

    Find the slope of the line through the points \((2,11)\) and \((7,1)\text{.}\)

    2.

    Write the equation of the line through the points \((2,11)\) and \((7,1)\) in Point-Slope Form.

    3.

    Write the equation of the line through the points \((2,11)\) and \((7,1)\) in Slope-Intercept Form.

    4.

    Write the equation of the line through the points \((2,11)\) and \((7,1)\) in Standard Form.

    5.

    Find the slope of the line represented by the equation \(\frac{1}{2}x - \frac{2}{3}y = 1\text{.}\)

    6.

    You and a friend used the same parking garage. You parked your car for 2 hours and it cost \(\$12\text{.}\) Your friend parked her car for 4 hours and it cost \(\$22\text{.}\)
    (a)
    Let \(x\) represent the number of hours of parking and let \(y\) represent the cost. Assuming the relationship is linear, how much does parking cost per hour?
    (b)
    Find a linear equation that models the cost of parking.
    (c)
    The garage charges a flat fee to enter the parking garage. How much is this fee?
