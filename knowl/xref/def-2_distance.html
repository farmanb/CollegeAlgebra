Definition 1.3.6. The Distance Formula.

The distance between the points \((x_1,y_1)\) and \((x_2,y_2)\) in the Cartesian plane is given by
\begin{equation*} \sqrt{(x_1 - x_2)^2 + (y_1 - y_2)^2}. \end{equation*}
This number measures the length of the line segment connecting the two points.
