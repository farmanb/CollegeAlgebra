Theorem 3.1.13. The Rational Root Theorem.

Assume
\begin{equation*} f(x) = a_nx^n + a_{n-1}x^{n-1} + \cdots + a_1x + a_0 \end{equation*}
with \(a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_n\) integers and \(a_n \neq 0\text{.}\) If \(p\) and \(q \neq 0\) are integers and \(r = p/q\) is a root of \(f\text{,}\) then \(p\) is a factor of \(a_0\) and \(q\) is a factor of \(a_n\text{.}\)
