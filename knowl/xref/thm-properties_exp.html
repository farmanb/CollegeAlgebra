Theorem 6.1.3. Properties of Exponential Functions.

Assume \(x\) and \(x^\prime\) are real numbers.
  1. \(\displaystyle \exp(0) = a^0 = 1.\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \exp(x + x^\prime) = \exp(x)\exp(x^\prime).\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \exp(x - x^\prime) = \frac{\exp(x)}{\exp(x^\prime)}.\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \exp(xx^\prime) = \exp(x)^{x^\prime} = \exp\left(x^\prime\right)^x.\)
